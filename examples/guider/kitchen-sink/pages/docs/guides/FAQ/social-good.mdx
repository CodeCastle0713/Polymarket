# How can Polymarket support social good?

By aggregating public opinion and insights, Polymarket offers valuable predictions on everything from elections to major global issues like climate change or public health crises. These insights can be used to better inform public policy, prepare for future challenges, and ensure that decision-making is based on real-time, data-driven forecasts rather than outdated or biased information.
